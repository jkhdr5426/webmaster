German-designed wearable barcode scanners for warehouses & industry

ProGlove creates compact, back-of-hand wearable scanners (MARK family) that let workers scan without interrupting their workflow. These devices are designed to improve ergonomics, speed, and accuracy for logistics and manufacturing operations.

Key product highlights (examples): multi-range scanning options, Bluetooth connectivity, and long battery life suitable for full shifts. (Sources: ProGlove product pages and reseller specs — see Reference Page.)

What is ProGlove?

ProGlove is a Munich, Germany company that builds wearable barcode scanners used by warehouses and manufacturing lines worldwide. Their wearable devices replace handheld scanners to allow hands-free scanning and faster, less error-prone workflows.
